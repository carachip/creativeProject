Kitchen Tips

Zacharie and I enjoy each others cooking habits as we both try very hard to follow good practices and maintain a well-taken care of and clean kitchen. That being said, we are both learning new things from one another about how that should be done. Here's a list of some ways to make sure the kitchen stays in best condition!

  • Never use the rag you use to clean the counters on the floor
  • Clean rags frequently. Don't be afraid to toss out your old sponge
  • Do not scoop out flour with the measuring cup (or worse, the liquid measuring container), rather, use a spoon
  • Rinse off food from used kitchen appliances immediately after use
  • Clean as you cook
  • Keep stovetop clean of crumbs and oil splatters
  • Avoid using metal on non-stick surfaces
  • Keep the lids of squeeze bottles clean
  • Use placemats and coasters (especially on wood)
  • Wipe off countertops after use
  • Store kitchen appliances in a orderly fashion
  • Keep floors clean
  • Don't be afraid to spend a little more to get something that will last
